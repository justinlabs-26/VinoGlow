Healthy Aging

Vino Glow.

Vino Glow is a premium grape-derived resveratrol ingredient sourced from the skins of red and purple grapes. Resveratrol is a naturally occurring polyphenol produced by grapes as a protective mechanism against environmental stressors. As a powerful antioxidant, it helps protect cells from oxidative stress caused by free radicals and supports overall wellness.

Renowned for its skin-supporting and healthy aging properties, Vino Glow helps defend the skin against environmental factors that can contribute to the visible signs of aging. By supporting the skin's natural antioxidant defenses, it promotes a more youthful, radiant appearance and helps maintain healthy-looking skin.

Vino Glow is valued for its ability to support skin vitality, improve the appearance of firmness and elasticity, and help maintain a smooth, even-toned complexion. Its antioxidant-rich profile supports the skin's natural renewal processes, making it an ideal ingredient for beauty, wellness, and healthy aging formulations focused on promoting long-term skin health and radiance.
